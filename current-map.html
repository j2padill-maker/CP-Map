📋 How to Use ▲

1. Upload your station PDF map.
2. Click Load Field Entries to pull in drop checks and bond checks.
3. Switch to Place mode and click the map to position each diamond pin.
4. Switch to Drag mode to reposition pins.
5. Hover a pin for quick info. Click for full detail.
6. Use Rectifier mode to place the ⚡ marker.
7. Use Arrow mode to drop flow arrows. Click to rotate 90°.
